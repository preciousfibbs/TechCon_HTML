Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup and has grown into one of the largest global tech conferences. Over the years, it has hosted events in over 20 countries and welcomed thousands of attendees.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire innovation, promote knowledge-sharing, and empower the next generation of technology leaders. We believe in building inclusive, diverse, and future-ready tech communities.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace (Virtual Speaker)

Recognized as the first computer programmer, Ada's theoretical work laid the foundation for modern algorithms.

Alan Turing (Virtual Speaker)

A pioneer of computer science and artificial intelligence, Alan's legacy influences the field of cryptography and computing today.

Grace Hopper

Known as the “Queen of Code,” Grace developed the first compiler and promoted machine-independent programming languages.
